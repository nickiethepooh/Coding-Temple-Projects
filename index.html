Welcome to the Woodshop

Drink amongst the barrels! Located next to our Bloomington Brewpub, the Woodshop is a state-of-the-art production facitity and event space where our sour ales are brewed, wood aged and packaged. Inspired by the place where craftsmen create and tinker, The Woodshop offers the ideal setting for any event!
