Book Your Event With Us

The Wood Shop is a fully operational brewery that transforms into a unique event venue when not in use for brewing. We primarily host private gatherings such as weddings, corporate conferences, presentations, and business dinners. Additionally, we occasionally open our doors to the public for select community events and experiences.

Types of Events We Can Host

Weddings

Celebrate your special day in a setting as memorable as your love story. The Wood Shop offers a warm, rustic atmosphere perfect for intimate ceremonies or grand receptions. With customizable layouts, local craft beverages, and a dedicated events team, we’ll help you create a wedding experience that’s uniquely yours.

Conferences/Presentations

Host your next conference, seminar, or presentation in a space that blends professionalism with personality. Our versatile event space is equipped to handle everything from keynote addresses to breakout sessions, with ample seating, A/V support, and refreshment options to keep your guests engaged and comfortable.

Business Dinner/Meetings

Impress clients or treat your team to a business dinner in an atmosphere that feels both refined and relaxed. Whether you’re celebrating milestones, discussing strategy, or simply unwinding after a big day, The Wood Shop offers curated menus, exceptional service, and the perfect setting for productive conversation.

Birthdays

Whether it’s a milestone celebration or a casual get-together, birthdays at The Wood Shop are unforgettable. With flexible space, a lively ambiance, and our signature craft brews on tap, we’ll help you throw a party that’s as fun and unique as the guest of honor.
